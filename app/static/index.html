Technical Analysis · —

PriceiEach candle shows the open, high, low and close for one period. Green = closed higher than it opened, red = closed lower. SMA 20iSimple Moving Average over the last 20 candles — the short-term average price. Reacts quickly to recent moves; price above it suggests short-term strength. SMA 50iSimple Moving Average over the last 50 candles — the medium-term trend. Slower than SMA 20; when SMA 20 crosses above SMA 50 it's often read as bullish. Bollinger bandsiTwo bands plotted 2 standard deviations above and below the 20-period average. They widen when the stock is volatile and narrow when calm; price near the upper band can mean overbought, near the lower band oversold. Projected targetiA dashed line from the current price to the pattern's measured-move target. An educational projection, not a guarantee. Support / ResistanceiDashed green = support (price floors), dashed red = resistance (ceilings). The S·n / R·n label shows how many times price touched that level — more touches = stronger. VolumeiBars at the bottom show shares traded per candle, coloured green (up day) / red (down day). Tall bars = high participation.
